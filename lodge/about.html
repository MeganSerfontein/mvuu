About Us

Activities

Game drives, fishing, canoeing and river cruises. Mvuu has a variety of activities for the whole family.

Friendly staff, great food, bar area, swimming pool all in a magnificent setting overlooking the Zambezi river

Chalets & Camping

Stone and thatch cottages, luxury safari tents, self catering cottages/tents and our famous camp sites ensure quality accommodation for all budgets.
